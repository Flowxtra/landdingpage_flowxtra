Job Ad Quality Guidelines

Last updated: March 8, 2025

These guidelines apply to all job postings published through Flowxtra, including listings hosted on Flowxtra subdomains, customer-connected domains, or any external job boards and social networks reached via our distribution tools. These guidelines also apply to all content posted through Flowxtra's Social Media Manager feature. By submitting a job or posting content through our Services, you agree to comply with the rules below in addition to our Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and applicable platform policies.

1. Anti-discrimination and respectful content

  • Job ads must not discriminate based on protected characteristics (such as age, gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, sexual orientation, marital status, or any classification protected under local law).
  • Avoid wording that implies a preference for a particular demographic group unless legally justified (for example, bona fide occupational requirements or government programmes).
  • Offensive, harassing, sexually explicit, or violent imagery/language is strictly prohibited.

2. Authentic and lawful opportunities

  • Listings must advertise genuine, legal roles. Flowxtra prohibits job scams, “get rich quick” offers, illicit services, or postings that encourage illegal conduct.
  • Multilevel marketing schemes, commission-only roles without clear pay structure, or positions requiring upfront fees must be transparently disclosed and may be rejected.
  • Freelance or unpaid roles (internships, volunteering, traineeships) must clearly indicate the compensation structure or lack thereof.

3. Promotional and spam controls

  • Flowxtra may remove postings that exist primarily to advertise products, drive traffic to other sites, or collect leads unrelated to a specific job opening.
  • Do not include multiple roles in a single posting. Each vacancy should have its own listing with unique responsibilities and requirements.
  • Excessive reposting or location “blasting” (posting the same job to many locations without real openings) is not allowed.

4. Transparency and accuracy

  • Provide clear job titles, responsibilities, required qualifications, employment type, and location. Avoid clickbait titles, unnecessary punctuation, or misleading claims.
  • Disclose the hiring company name (or state clearly if a recruiting agency is acting on behalf of a client). Generic or “confidential” postings may be rejected unless lawfully justified.
  • If you collect applications through Flowxtra, do not redirect candidates to external job boards or forms unless the integration is explicitly supported.

5. Candidate data and security

  • Never request sensitive personal data (e.g., national ID numbers, bank details) in the job description or initial application unless legally required and appropriately protected.
  • Do not request payments from candidates for applications, background checks, or training materials. Flowxtra reserves the right to remove any listing that demands money or goods from applicants.
  • All processing of candidate information must comply with your applicable privacy laws and our Data Processing Agreement.

6. Compliance with platform and local regulations

  • When Flowxtra distributes your job to partners (LinkedIn, Meta, Instagram, X/Twitter, Google Jobs, Snapchat, Discord communities, WhatsApp Channels, Telegram, or other regional job boards and social destinations), you must follow each platform's advertising rules. Flowxtra may edit, pause, or remove the ad if it violates partner policies.
  • You are responsible for complying with all applicable labor, advertising, and consumer protection laws in the jurisdictions where you recruit, including wage-transparency and equal opportunity requirements.

7. Social Media Manager and content posting

Flowxtra provides Social Media Manager tools that allow you to publish posts and content to platforms such as Meta (Facebook and Instagram), LinkedIn, X (formerly Twitter), Google Business Profiles, Snapchat, TikTok, Discord, WhatsApp, Telegram, and other integrated networks. When using these features, you must comply with the following requirements:

  • Platform-specific policies: You are responsible for ensuring that all content posted through Flowxtra's Social Media Manager complies with each platform's terms of service, community guidelines, and advertising policies. This includes restrictions on prohibited content, age restrictions, and platform-specific advertising rules.
  • Content accuracy and authenticity: All posts must be truthful, accurate, and not misleading. Do not use Social Media Manager to publish false information, impersonate others, or engage in deceptive practices.
  • Prohibited content: You must not use Social Media Manager to post content that is illegal, discriminatory, harassing, defamatory, obscene, or violates intellectual property rights. This includes but is not limited to hate speech, spam, phishing attempts, or content that promotes illegal activities.
  • Job-related content: While Social Media Manager can be used for various recruitment-related posts, ensure that job postings shared via social media comply with all sections of these guidelines, including anti-discrimination requirements and transparency standards.
  • Third-party rights: You must have the necessary rights and permissions to post any content, including images, videos, text, or other materials. Do not post content that infringes on copyrights, trademarks, privacy rights, or other intellectual property of third parties.
  • Data protection: When posting content that includes personal information of candidates, employees, or other individuals, ensure you have appropriate consent and comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and our Data Processing Agreement.
  • Account security: You are responsible for maintaining the security of your social media account credentials connected to Flowxtra. Do not share credentials with unauthorized parties, and notify Flowxtra immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.
  • Platform violations: If a social media platform removes, restricts, or flags content posted through Flowxtra's Social Media Manager due to policy violations, Flowxtra may suspend your access to Social Media Manager features or take other appropriate action.

Flowxtra reserves the right to monitor, review, and remove content posted through Social Media Manager if it violates these guidelines, platform policies, or applicable laws. Repeated violations may result in permanent suspension of Social Media Manager access.

8. Enforcement

Flowxtra monitors job ads and social media content for quality and compliance. We may remove content, suspend job distribution, suspend Social Media Manager access, or deactivate accounts that fail to meet these standards. Repeated or serious violations may lead to permanent suspension. If you believe content was removed in error, contact office@flowxtra.com with supporting details.
