HOW DO CUSTOM DOMAINS AND JOB DISTRIBUTION WORK?

Flowxtra enables employers to connect their own domains or Flowxtra-hosted subdomains to publish job listings. When a customer uses this feature, the customer is solely responsible for the accuracy, legality, and compliance of each posting, including adherence to local labor laws, advertising rules, and platform terms of service.

At the customer's instruction, Flowxtra may syndicate job listings and related metadata to external job boards or social networks (for example LinkedIn, Meta/Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, Google Jobs, Snapchat, Discord servers, WhatsApp Channels, Telegram, or other regional aggregators). Flowxtra also provides Social Media Manager tools that allow customers to publish posts and content directly to these and other social media platforms (including TikTok, Google Business Profiles, and similar destinations). These third-party channels have their own policies, and the customer must ensure that each posting and social media content complies with those requirements. Flowxtra may edit, pause, or refuse to distribute any job advertisement or social media content that violates a partner platform's policies or our Legal Terms.

When job distribution is enabled, we process and share the content of the job listing (title, description, location, salary range, hiring company details, and contact methods) with the selected platforms. If a customer chooses to collect applications directly on those platforms, the partner may also receive candidate information. The lawful basis for this processing is the performance of the contract with the customer (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR) and, where required, the explicit consent of the candidate. Customers must provide any required notices or obtain consent from candidates before enabling third-party distribution.

The following table summarises the respective responsibilities:

Area Flowxtra Customer
Infrastructure Provides secure hosting, APIs, and distribution tooling. Configures DNS/SSL, ensures branding and assets respect third-party rights.
Job content Transmits postings exactly as supplied, with optional formatting. Guarantees accuracy, legality, wage transparency, and equal-opportunity statements.
Regulatory compliance Implements global privacy and security safeguards. Complies with labor, advertising, and data-protection laws for each target jurisdiction.
Candidate data Processes applications per the DPA and customer instructions. Provides required notices/consents before enabling third-party distribution.
Disputes Co-operates where legally required. Handles claims from regulators, candidates, or platforms arising from their postings.

All customers must also follow the Flowxtra Job Ad Quality Guidelines to ensure postings remain compliant with content, anti-spam, and platform-specific rules.

6. DO WE OFFER ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE-BASED PRODUCTS?

In Short: We use AI to assist with recruitment workflows—not to make hiring decisions. All employment decisions remain under full human control by the hiring organization.

Our Services include AI-powered features ("AI Products") designed to streamline recruitment processes and improve efficiency. Flowxtra is a human-first Applicant Tracking System (ATS) that complies with the EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689), GDPR, and applicable employment laws.

What Our AI Does

Flowxtra's AI is used exclusively for the following recruitment support tasks:

  • Job posting creation: AI assists HR teams in drafting and optimizing job descriptions and social media posts
  • Communication assistance: AI helps generate email templates and responses to streamline candidate communications
  • Job distribution automation: AI automates posting jobs to platforms like LinkedIn, Google Jobs, and other job boards
  • Data search and filtering: AI helps employers search their candidate database faster (e.g., finding candidates with specific skills like "PHP developer" or "cybersecurity experience")
  • Translation services: AI translates job postings and communications across multiple languages

What Our AI Does NOT Do

Important: Flowxtra does not use AI for automated decision-making in hiring. Specifically, our AI does not:

  • Score, rank, or rate candidates
  • Recommend or select "the best candidate"
  • Perform automated profiling, matching, or filtering of applicants
  • Make or influence hiring, interview, or rejection decisions
  • Access or process sensitive personal data such as photos, gender, nationality, ethnicity, religion, or place of birth

All hiring decisions—including who to interview, shortlist, or hire—are made exclusively by human recruiters or HR professionals within the hiring organization. Flowxtra's AI serves only as a productivity tool to accelerate administrative tasks.

Data Isolation and Multi-Tenant Architecture

Flowxtra operates a multi-tenant system where each company has its own isolated database. Candidate data (including names, emails, CVs, and applications) is accessible only to the hiring organization that posted the job. Flowxtra does not access, view, or analyze individual candidate data except for:

  • Job posting title and URL (to monitor compliance with Austrian, EU, US, Canadian, and Australian employment laws)
  • Technical support requests explicitly initiated by the customer

Our AI does not train on your candidate data, and we do not use candidate information for any purpose beyond providing the Services to your organization.

Compliance with EU AI Act and GDPR

Based on guidance from Austria's regulatory authority (RTR KI-Servicestelle), Flowxtra is not classified as a high-risk AI system under the EU AI Act because:

  • We do not perform automated decision-making or candidate filtering
  • Our AI is used only to assist, not to decide
  • We do not process sensitive personal data (Article 9 GDPR categories)
  • All AI interactions are transparent and disclosed to users

Flowxtra complies with Article 50 of the EU AI Act regarding transparency requirements. For more information, see the official sources:

  • EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689)
  • RTR Austria – Transparency Requirements
