Flowxtra Sub-processors

Flowxtra GmbH engages carefully selected sub-processors to support its infrastructure, communications, and payment capabilities. Each provider is bound by a written agreement with data protection clauses that meet Article 28 GDPR requirements. When a provider stores or accesses data outside the EEA, Flowxtra relies on Standard Contractual Clauses or another valid transfer mechanism and applies appropriate safeguards.

Social platforms connected through Flowxtra's Social Media Manager (such as Meta/Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Google Business Profiles, Snapchat, TikTok, Discord, WhatsApp, or Telegram) typically act as independent controllers when you publish content through their APIs and therefore are not listed as sub-processors here. Customers should review those platforms' privacy terms to understand how they process data associated with shared posts or campaigns.
