# Flowxtra - Pricing & Features Documentation

Flowxtra is a comprehensive recruitment and social media management platform that combines ATS (Applicant Tracking System) functionality with powerful social media scheduling tools. Our platform helps businesses streamline their hiring process while managing their social media presence effectively.

## Detailed Feature Descriptions

### Recruitment Features

#### Job Postings
- **Description:** Number of active job slots you can publish simultaneously per month
- **Available in:** All plans (Unlimited)

#### Google Job Listing
- **Description:** Automatically list your jobs on Google for Jobs for free. Increase visibility and reach more candidates through Google Search without additional costs.
- **Available in:** All plans

#### Career Page
- **Description:** Your branded public careers page listing all open roles.
- **Available in:** All plans

#### Job Widget
- **Description:** Embed your job list on any website with a copy-paste snippet.
- **Available in:** All plans

#### Multiposting
- **Description:** Distribute job ads to multiple channels simultaneously for maximum exposure. Includes Google Jobs, LinkedIn, and other platforms.
- **Available in:** All plans

#### Kanban Board
- **Description:** Kanban-style board to visualize and move candidates through hiring stages. Simple column view for quick candidate management.
- **Available in:** All plans

#### Diagram Board
- **Description:** Advanced diagram board with drag-and-drop functionality, detailed activity history, and comprehensive candidate tracking. Enhanced visualization with filters and analytics.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### CV Parsing
- **Description:** Automatically extract and structure candidate information from CVs and resumes. Supports multiple file formats including PDF, Word, and text files.
- **Available in:** All plans

#### Smart Candidate Filtering
- **Description:** Auto-prioritizes applicants based on role requirements and custom criteria.
- **Available in:** Starter and above

#### Skills Reservoir
- **Description:** Store and organize top candidates who didn't get hired for future opportunities. Build a database of qualified talent ready for upcoming positions that match their skills.
- **Available in:** Basic and above

#### Talent Pool
- **Description:** Maintain a database of silver-medalist and future-fit candidates.
- **Available in:** Advanced and above

#### Candidate Presentation
- **Description:** Generate secure shareable links to present candidate profiles with your notes and comments to clients, managers, or external partners.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### Candidate Import
- **Description:** Import candidate data from CSV, Excel, and other ATS systems. Bulk upload candidates with automatic data mapping and validation.
- **Available in:** Advanced and above

#### Candidate Export
- **Description:** Export lists and reports for external analysis and backups. Supports CSV and Excel formats.
- **Available in:** Advanced and above

### Social Media Features

#### Social Media Scheduling
- **Description:** Schedule and manage your social media posts across all platforms. Plan your content in advance and maintain a consistent posting schedule.
- **Available in:** All plans

#### Social Media Accounts
- **Description:** Number of social media accounts you can connect and manage through the platform.
- **Available in:** All plans

#### Social Media Posts
- **Description:** Schedule and publish unlimited posts, stories, videos, and any content across all social media platforms. Full content scheduling and management system.
- **Available in:** Enterprise plan (Unlimited)

#### Social Media Pipeline
- **Description:** Build automated stages for promoting content across multiple channels, track progress, and coordinate hand-offs between marketing and recruitment teams.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### Social Ads Manager
- **Description:** Create and manage job advertisements across major platforms including Facebook, Instagram, SnapChat, Google, YouTube, TikTok, and LinkedIn.
- **Available in:** Advanced and above

### Customization Features

#### Custom Fields
- **Description:** Add any fields you need to structure job data and forms.
- **Available in:** Starter and above

#### Custom Email SMTP
- **Description:** Connect your company's SMTP server to send emails directly from your own email address through our platform.
- **Available in:** Starter and above

#### Remove Flowxtra Branding
- **Description:** Remove Flowxtra logo and branding from your career page. Display your company branding only.
- **Available in:** Basic and above

#### Brand Kit
- **Description:** Customize your entire system with your brand colors, fonts, and logo. Your career pages, emails, and all candidate-facing content will reflect your brand identity consistently.
- **Available in:** Basic and above

#### Custom Career Page
- **Description:** Build rich careers pages with sections for culture, benefits, and teams. Supports multi-section layouts.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### Custom Domain
- **Description:** Use your own domain for careers pages and application forms.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### Company Page
- **Description:** Get a branded subdomain with your company profile and jobs.
- **Available in:** All plans

### Workflow & Automation

#### Journey Flow
- **Description:** Advanced workflow automation builder (like n8n). Create custom recruitment processes with conditional paths based on candidate responses. Auto-send welcome emails, request additional info, send contracts, training courses, and create dynamic workflows with triggers, wait times, conditions, and multiple branches.
- **Available in:** Basic and above

#### Automated Messages
- **Description:** Send automatic confirmations, rejections, and reminders to candidates.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### Email Template Builder
- **Description:** Design and customize email templates for all candidate communications. Create branded, professional emails with your own design, layout, and messaging.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### Newsletter
- **Description:** Send automated job alerts to candidates who opted-in during application. Notify them about new opportunities via email, SMS, WhatsApp, or Telegram. Includes Mailchimp integration for advanced campaigns.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

### Team & Collaboration

#### User Seats
- **Description:** Total team members who can collaborate inside your account.
- **Available in:** All plans (varies by plan)

#### Roles & Permissions
- **Description:** Granular access control for recruiters, hiring managers, and admins.
- **Available in:** Starter and above

#### Multi Office Location
- **Description:** Organize jobs, users, and reports by location. Support for multi-location companies.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### Client Profiles
- **Description:** Manage multiple client companies with separate jobs and teams. Perfect for agencies.
- **Available in:** Basic and above

### Compliance & Security

#### GDPR & CCPA Compliance
- **Description:** Full compliance with GDPR (European Union) and CCPA (California) data protection regulations. Includes data subject rights management, consent tracking, data portability, right to erasure, privacy controls, and automated compliance reports.
- **Available in:** All plans

#### Accessibility WCAG
- **Description:** Full compliance with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Ensures your career pages and application forms are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.
- **Available in:** Starter and above

#### Policy Generator
- **Description:** Create customized privacy policies tailored to your needs. Choose from pre-built templates for different privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) and customize the text to match your company requirements.
- **Available in:** Starter and above

#### Two-Factor Authentication
- **Description:** Protect accounts with an extra verification step at login. Also known as 2FA.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### SSL Certificate
- **Description:** Free SSL certificate for your subdomain. Every company gets a free subdomain from Flowxtra, and each subdomain receives a free SSL certificate from us to ensure secure connections.
- **Available in:** All plans

### Integrations

- **Description:** Plug-and-play integrations with popular HR, comms, and analytics tools.
- **Available in:** Starter and above

#### ATS Integrations
- **Description:** Connect Flowxtra with your HR stack, websites, and internal tools.
- **Available in:** Professional and above

#### CRM Integration

- **Available in:** Enterprise plan

#### API/MCP

#### Custom LLMs Integration
- **Description:** Integrate your own LLMs that are hosted on your infrastructure. We provide the integration tools (API & MCP) so your AI models can work seamlessly within Flowxtra while staying in your environment.
- **Available in:** Enterprise plan
